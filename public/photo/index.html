How did I start?

When my dad gave me a Minolta X-270 in 9th grade.

Did I study photography?

While I majored in photography at the Savannah College of Art and Design, I really consider myself self-taught.

Do I freelance?

Possibly, yes. Thus far, I've avoiding having any ambition for my photography other than taking cool images. It keeps things pure.

Is my work in galleries?

I've participated in one or two shows, but exhibition has really been tangential to my aims as a photographer.

At some point, though, I'd like to have a solo show.

Do I only shoot digital?

Yes. I will always hold a special place in my heart for film… but the future is inescapably digital.
